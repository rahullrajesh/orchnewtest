Awareness & Sensitization

Online workshops by Neuro diverse team of facilitators to enhance awareness & empathy through sharing of lived experiences.

These include self-assessments of neurodivergent traits, and a shift to the social model, with a focus on strengths.

They emphasize the “what’s in it for me” to employees and identify who would be interested in forming a core ND Employee Support Group.

ND Integration Workshop

Online Focus groups with ESG Members, Team Members, Team Leads and Managers of ND Hires. These ensure safe work environments.

Are conducted by ND Cofacilitators. These provide existing employees the opportunity to work in neurodiverse teams on simulation projects.

These not only offer hands on experience of working with ND team members but also allow identification of process gaps with SOPs in place for an inclusive workplace.

Hiring

Competency mapping of targeted roles and identification of Behavioural Indicators, followed by design of inclusive Job Descriptions.

Competency assessment tools designed to assess and screen ND individuals.

Partner with Hiring companies to customize and facilitate inclusive hiring process and identify the right fit.

Coaching

One on one and group coaching interventions with ND and NT employees to ensure all members are able to contribute to their fullest potential enabling win-win situations for all stakeholders.

Individual Development Plans are laid down for ND employees, progress reviewed, and feedback is shared with employees and their managers.

I realized neurodiversity was only a variation in the thinking pattern of the brain. I could relate to so many neurodivergent traits like keeping my workstation neat and orderly. When such traits are in us, we find them 'normal' and yet when we see it in some others we give labels and they become 'not normal'.

Listening to people sharing their lived experiences taught me a lot. For the first time was able to relate to the difficulties they experience. For the first time I understood that a “happy” scenario for me may not be happy for them.
